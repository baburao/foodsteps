Our Story

About Us

"We believe every baby deserves a head start with food that's as pure as their smile."

At FoodSteps, we're on a mission to make healthy, enjoyable and nutritious food purees accessible for every parent — whether you're a busy professional or a dedicated stay-at-home mum or dad.

Every pouch we make is a labour of love. We source the finest natural ingredients, blend them to a perfectly smooth consistency, and pack them in compact, ready-to-eat pouches — so you can focus on the moments that matter.

FoodSteps is a product of Xobu Food & Beverages Pvt Ltd, based in Tumkur, Karnataka, India.
